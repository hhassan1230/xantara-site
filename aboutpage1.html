Olga Andreyeva
I’m an independent illustrator and comic creator living in New York City. After graduating from Pratt Institute, I have been keeping busy by creating Xantara, illustrating personal and commissioned pieces, and working with various video game studios as an artist. I’m deeply passionate about storytelling, art, animation and games and always striving to improve my work.
